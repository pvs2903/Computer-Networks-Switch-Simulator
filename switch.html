📡 How It Works:

Click on any device to select it as sender, then click another device to send data!

Switch Functions - Click to Demonstrate:

  • 📚 Learning: Switch discovers and remembers device MAC addresses and port locations
  • 🌊 Flooding: When destination is unknown, data is sent to all ports except the sender
  • 📤 Forwarding: Direct communication between known devices using MAC table
  • 🚫 Filtering: Prevents unnecessary traffic by blocking data on unused ports
